Located in central Lucknow and conceptualized as a sprawling traditional
bungalow with a lush green lawn, lebua Lucknow is a luxury boutique
property that reflects and personifies the Art Deco architecture prevailing
in the area in the early 1900s.

Explore the history and heritage of a bygone era, lapped up in luxury.

The heritage bungalow has been lovingly restored to its former glory
– a blend of modern hospitality and a traditional ethos that ensures
each guest is made to feel part of the story, along with the rich
“Ganga-Jamuna” culture of Lucknow.

All 41 rooms feature stunning pieces of art, reflecting quintessential
Lakhnavi heritage of living spaces opening onto courtyards and
terraces that evoke an era when Lucknow was synonymous with an
ostentatious lifestyle, nurtured by its sprawling ‘kothis’ and ‘havelis’.
The resort’s ambience and hospitality deliver the perfect Lucknow
experience, making for a perfect retreat to the wonderful ‘City of
Illusions’.
